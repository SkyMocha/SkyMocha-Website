RXNS

A Discord Bot who uses complicated methods to do a simple thing.

RXNS WEBSITE

Although FuzzBot was my first Discord bot, at the time I did not have the knowledge to make it what I envisoned it to be, a bot that could easily provide customizable reaction images. Now with far more knowledge in coding, I was able to make RXNS, a bot that provides customizable server-based reactions. RXNS does this on a custom website that feeds off of the Discord API, the RXNS API that I built, and a MongoDB database.

Over the span of a few weeks I built up RXNS and continued modifying it until it was ready for production. After a few weeks of waiting, RXNS was approved on top.gg and instantly gained a small following of 40, then 60, then 80 servers. Over time that has slowly settled to around 60, but RXNS proves to still be one of my best bots to date!
