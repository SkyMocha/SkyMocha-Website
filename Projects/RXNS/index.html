RXNS (Reactions)

A Discord Bot who uses complicated methods to do a simple thing.

Overview

Although FuzzBot was my first Discord bot, at the time I did not have the knowledge to make it what I envisoned it to be, a bot that could easily provide customizable reaction images. FuzzBots original purpose, was to make reacting to peoples messages with an image far easier. For example, if someone said "oh worm?" a common phrase in a server I was in, a drawing made by a friend would show up in response. Now with far more knowledge in coding, I was able to make RXNS, a bot that provides customizable server-based reactions. RXNS does this through a custom website that feeds off of the Discord API, the RXNS API that I built, and a MongoDB database.

Process

Over the span of a few weeks I built up RXNS and continued modifying it until it was ready for production. Once done, and after a few weeks of waiting, RXNS was approved on top.gg and instantly gained a small following of 40, then 60, then 80 servers. By the end of its life-span, RXNS had around 60 servers, throughout recieving small updates to the web dashboard, and back-end.
