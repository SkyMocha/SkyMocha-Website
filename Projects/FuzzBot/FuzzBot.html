About
FuzzBot is a multipurpose Discord bot whose primary function is to provide fun commands, as well as some utility to a server.

The bot has been in on and off development since December 2017. Over this time Nick/AntarcticRuler has been able to experiment and add commands to create a bot that will improve any server.

FuzzBot will also send a welcome message to all new members of a server, and a farewell message to those who leave the server.

Any questions about FuzzBot can be sent to AntarcticRuler#1529, or to FuzzBot himself. You can also contact Nick on his Discord Server.

FuzzBot is written in Node.js using the Discord.js package, with pulls from many different APIs.
Images
f!images; f!cat; f!dog
FuzzBot provides an assortment of images that can spice up conversation.

There are specific commands such as the f!cat / f!dog, however, the main use of FuzzBot are it's reaction images. For example, type ok! In the chat, and you'll receive a thumbs up reaction from FuzzBot.

If you have any ideas for reaction images, reach out to AntarcitcRuler#1529 or DM FuzzBot a link to the image!
Emojis
f!emojis; -emoji name-
FuzzBot provides a handful of emojis that are readily accessible to users. The emojis list holds both still images as well as gifs.

f!emojis will give a list of all the emojis you can use.

To use an emoji from the list, just type in chat the name of the emoji surrounded by two dashes

Ie. -ping-
Dice
f!d [number]
Randomized events just got a lot easier! FuzzBot is highly capable of rolling dice so you don't have to.

To roll a dice just type f!d [number]

Ie. f!d 6
Coin Flip
f!cp; f!cf
Ever needed to settle something online with a simple coin flip? Well the other person can't see the coin, but they can see FuzzBot's message.

To flip a coin just type f!cp or f!cf

ps. cp is a typo FuzzBot made a while ago, but decided to stick with.
Quiz
f!quiz [category] [difficulty]; f!quizcat
Want to prove your knowledge? Using the Open Trivia Database, FuzzBot is able to deliver quizzes on just about any subject!

f!quiz will give a quiz in a random category at a random difficulty
f!quiz [category] gives a quiz from a specific category at a random difficulty
f!quiz [category] [difficulty] delivers a quiz from a specific category at a specific difficulty

To see all categories, type f!quizcat
Urban Dictionary
f!ud [term]; f!rud
No longer do you have to google that phrase in urban dictionary yourself! FuzzBot will do it for you.

f!ud [term] replies with the definition of the term as well as an example sentence.

f!rud will give a random urban dictionary term.
Random Quotes & Movie Quotes
f!quote; f!mquote
“FuzzBot chooses to deliver quotes, not because it is easy, but because it is fun”– or something like that.

Using Andruxnet's random quote API, FuzzBot is packed with quotes he can show you.

f!quote is for general quotes

f!mquote is for movie specific quotes
That Really Blanked My Blank
f!trbmb; f!blank
Using the TRBMB API, FuzzBot can return funny and sometimes outrageous That Really ____ My ____ statements!

f!blank or f!trbmb will return a random That Really Blanked My Blank statement
Distance
f!dist x1,y1 x2,y2
FuzzBot can hopefully make your math homework a little bit easier!

f!dist followed by two coordinates num,num will return the distance

Ie. f!dist 0,0 0,5 will return 5 as the distance between point one and point two
Kick & Ban
f!kick/ban [mention] [reason]
FuzzBot isn't just a bot for fun, he can sometimes get down to business too.

f!kick [mention] [reason] will kick someone.

The person will be notified the reason as to why they were kicked, and the channel you send the command in will be informed of the kicking as well.

To ban someone, just type f!ban [mention] [reason]

Ie. f!kick @AntarcticRuler#1529 you have committed crimes against NickStudios HQ

*You need the ban members permission for both commands
Server & FuzzBot Statistics
f!stats; f!fuzzstats
An easy way to check your basic server statistics is provided by FuzzBot!

Upon f!stats, the server name, icon, member count, owner, region, and ID will be returned in a nice embed.

If you wish to check up on FuzzBot, f!fuzzstats will return FuzzBot's statistics.
