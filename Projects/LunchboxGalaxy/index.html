LunchBox Galaxy

A simple game-jam game with potential

Overview

After missing the opportunity to compete in my second Ludum Dare, I decided the next best thing would be to record myself competing in MiniJam, a bi-weekly game jam.

My project would be a simple game where you move around in a small solar system. After making my first planet based on Earth, I found that one of the continents looked very much like an avocado, so I decided to name the planet Avocado. After that, I began naming and also designing my planets around certain foods.

This project was extremely insightful for me. Although it did not involve much programming, I did do all the artwork. The artwork was simple pixel art, yet, art ended up being my highest rating in the jam. Pixel art, when given character and emotion can carry a project or game.
