Endowing an AI with Theory of Mind using cognitive modeling

Overview

Peddie has a program where junior year we are assisted with finding university labs to work at. This program is called STEM EXP. I participated in the computer science part, and recieved help in cold-calling labs. After my third email, I recieved a response from the GUII lab at University of California Santa Cruz, a HCI lab dedicated to working with video games.
