You're. Not. Supposed. To. Talk. About. It!

Overview

My High School Senior Thesis for The Peddie School (Colloqium Unavailable) later turned YouTube video.

7,000 words long, this paper is an in-depth analysis of Fight Club and it's implications in the real world.

The paper details Tyler Durden as a populist leader using a populous' disalusionment with corporate America and a sense of emmasculation in order to build an army, then proceeding to explore real-world parallels with the incel & manosphere communities, as well as their use within real-world campaigns exampled by Steve Bannon's use of Incels as "early recruits in [Trump's] future insurgency." (Mindf*ck, 152)

Lenses of: literary analysis of Fight Club, academic research & contemporary history (SPLC, ADL, APA), comparisons (Jonestown, 1984, Mindf*ck, The Matrix, Joker), outside perspectives (Pete C. Baker, ContraPoints, Shaun); are used in order to find Fight Club's place in our society.
