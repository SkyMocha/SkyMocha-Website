Geiger Reeevs Competition One & Two

A speech competition I participated in Freshmen & Junior year, winning hearts and awards

Overview

The Peddie School has two yearly speech competetions, a monologue competition (The Declemation), and a written speech competition (Geiger Reeves). I participated two times, once in my freshmen year, and another in my Junior year.

Freshmen Reeevs - The Courage To Ask

I knew most people would look within themselves and find a deep and meaningfull topic to convey to an audiance whether to win the competition, or to spread a message. I took this oppourtunity to look within myself, and write a speech about my love life. Although I won nothing for this speech, I won crowd favorite, recieving weeks of congratulations, and a standing ovation.

The Courage To Ask - Audio Only

Junior Reeevs - I'm weird and so are you

My junior year I strived to have some balance between light-heartnedness and an important message. Therefore, I talked about something that many high-school students struggle with: being considered "weird". This speech "I'm weird and so are you" is a speech centered around celebrating weirdness. Due to COVID the speeches were not delivered on stage, but recorded in video. For this speech I recieved honorable mention out of approx. 30 competitors.
